lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich fillings such as meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel or tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Meat sauce
  • Cheese
  • Béchamel sauce

Steps

  1. 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. 1 onion (chopped)
  3. 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  4. 500g (1 lb) ground beef or sausage
  5. 1 can (400g / 14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  6. 2 tbsp tomato paste
  7. 1 tsp dried basil
  8. 1 tsp dried oregano
  9. Salt and pepper to taste
